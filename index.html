Why This Archive Matters

Kenya has produced numerous high-quality reports with detailed recommendations for national transformation. Many of these reports were commissioned at great public expense but were never fully implemented. This archive serves as a permanent, accessible record of these critical documents.

Key Reports in Our Archive

Kriegler Report (2008)

Investigation into the 2007/2008 post-election violence and electoral failures with comprehensive recommendations for electoral reforms.
