National Dialogue Committee

NADCO Report (2023)

Released: November 2023 132 pages Co-chairs: Kalonzo Musyoka & Kimani Ichung'wah

Executive Summary

The NADCO Report, officially titled "Report of the National Dialogue Committee on the Bipartisan Talks", was established following the 2022/23 political crisis and anti-government protests. The committee comprised representatives from both government and opposition coalitions.

Core Mandate:

  • Address cost of living concerns
  • Review electoral justice and audit 2022 elections
  • Establish fidelity to political parties/coalitions
  • Create inclusive governance structure
  • Address national cohesion issues

Key Findings

Fig 2: 2022 election audit findings (Source: NADCO Technical Committee)

1. Cost of Living Crisis

The committee identified urgent economic pressures:

  • 83% of Kenyans reported reduced purchasing power
  • Food inflation peaked at 13.8% in 2023
  • Fuel prices increased by 45% since 2022
  • 72% of households cutting essential expenses

2. Electoral Reforms

Key issues in electoral management:

  • Voter register contained 164,000 discrepancies
  • Results transmission delays in 12% of stations
  • Need for stronger IEBC selection mechanism
  • Legal gaps in presidential election dispute resolution

3. Governance Structure

Proposed constitutional changes:

  • Establishment of Official Leader of Opposition office
  • 50% budgetary allocation to county governments
  • Two-thirds gender rule implementation mechanism
  • Expanded executive (Prime Minister + 2 Deputies)

Key Recommendations

01

Economic Recovery

Immediate reduction of VAT on fuel from 16% to 8%

02

Electoral Reforms

Constitutional amendment to restructure IEBC selection panel

03

Opposition Office

Establish official Leader of Opposition with state funding

04

Constitutional Review

Referendum on expanded executive structure by 2025

Implementation Status (2024 Update)

Not Implemented (60%)
In Progress (30%)
Complete (10%)

Notable Developments:

  • Complete: VAT on fuel reduced to 8% (Oct 2023)
  • In Progress: IEBC selection panel reconstitution bill tabled
  • Not Implemented: No action on Leader of Opposition position
  • Not Implemented: Referendum plans stalled
